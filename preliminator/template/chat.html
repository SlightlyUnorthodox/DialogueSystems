Compling Chatbot
Instructions
Starting Compling Chatbot
  • Press enter to start the demo or by speaking into the microphone.
Using the Chatbot
  • You may interact with the system by typing your response or speaking directly into the mic.
  • You can toggle speech synthesis on or off by clicking on the microphone button at the top of the page.
  • Speak or type, "back", at any time to go back to the previous utterance.
Ending the Chatbot
  • To end your session, please speak or enter "quit", and click on the provided link.
